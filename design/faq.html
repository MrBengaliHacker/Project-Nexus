Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I register for an event?

You can go to the Events page and click on the event you want to register for.

Q2: Where can I view my attendance?

Go to the Attendance page from the dashboard to see your records.

Q3: How do I submit feedback?

Click on the Feedback section in the dashboard and fill out the form provided.

Q4: Who can I contact for technical issues?

You can contact your club admin or email support@example.com.
